Income Tax - Income Tax

Domestic taxes can be classified into direct taxes and indirect taxes. Direct tax is a tax paid directly to the government on your income.

Indirect taxes are taxes levied on you and paid to the government by someone else on your behalf. For example, hotels, movie theaters, etc. fall into this category.

They will collect the taxes you owe on the products or services you purchase and pay them to the government on your behalf. Direct taxes can be classified into two types, income tax and corporate tax.

Income tax- Individuals or non-Hindu family members (HUFs) or companies other than taxpayers are liable to pay income tax on their own income. The law dictates how much tax should be paid on how much income. Corporate tax - The tax that companies pay on their profits is treated as corporate tax. Relevant laws dictate the amount of tax that must be paid.

Earnings vary ..

Income can be of many types. Salary, pension or savings accounts, interest on fixed deposits, rent on leased property, funds, cash on delivery of shares, business, cash, professional income, etc., all fall under income. Eventually the winners of events like Kaun Banega Crorepati will also have to pay income tax on the prize amount.
